Balogna Cake Recipe

Home Page

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of sliced bologna
  • 2 tablespoons grated onion
  • 1 8-oz package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
  • 1 package of crackers

Directions:

  1. Mix softened cream cheese, onion and Worcestershire sauce together in a bowl. Beat until fluffy.
  2. Place a slice of bologna on a plate and spread the cheese mix on its surface.
  3. Continue adding alternating layers of bologna and cheese mix until all of the bologna is used.
  4. Spread the remaining cheese mix smoothly around the sides and top of the cake.
  5. Cool in refrigerator. To serve, cut into very thin slices like a traditional cake and place the slices on crackers.
