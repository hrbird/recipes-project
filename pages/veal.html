Freezer-to-Oven Veal Dinner

Home Page

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground veal
  • 1/2 cup tomato juice
  • 1/4 cup dehydrated onion flakes
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 4 radish slices
  • Parsley sprigs

Directions:

  1. Combine veal, tomato juice, onion flakes, Worcestershire, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Mix well.
  2. Use 2/3 of the veal mixture to form a loaf.
  3. Spread mushrooms on the loaf in an even layer, up to 1/2-inch from each edge.
  4. Top with the remaining veal mixture. Mold firmly, being sure the veal totally covers the mushrooms.
  5. Wrap in freezer wrap and freeze until needed.
  6. To cook, thaw the veal loaf in refrigerator for a few hours.
  7. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  8. Bake veal loaf on a rack for 1 hour, or until well done.
  9. Arrange on a serving platter and garnish with radish slices and parsley sprigs.
  10. Serve warm. Makes 4 dinner servings.
